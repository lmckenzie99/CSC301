# Language Grammars
- Rules for building parse trees or derivation trees for sentences in the language defined by the grammar  
<Sentence>*    ::= <Noun-Phrase> <Verb> <Noun-Phrase>
<Noun-Phrase>  ::= <Article> <Noun>
<Verb>         ::= loves | hates | eats
<Article>      ::= a | the
<Noun>         ::= dog | cat | rat
